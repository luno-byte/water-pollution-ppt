What is Water Pollution?

Water pollution occurs when contaminants are introduced into water bodies, making them unfit for consumption or other purposes.

  • Chemical substances (heavy metals, pesticides)
  • Microorganisms (bacteria, viruses)
  • Waste materials (plastic, organic matter)
  • Thermal and physical contaminants

Major Causes of Water Pollution

  • Industrial Discharge - Factories releasing chemicals
  • Agricultural Runoff - Fertilizers and pesticides
  • Untreated Sewage - Domestic waste
  • Plastic Pollution - Single-use plastics
  • Oil Spills - Fuel leakage
  • Urban Runoff - City waste and chemicals

Solutions and Prevention

  • Wastewater Treatment - Primary, secondary, tertiary
  • Industrial Regulation - Compliance and monitoring
  • Sustainable Agriculture - Reduced chemical use
  • Plastic Waste Reduction - Ban single-use plastics
  • Water Conservation - Efficient water use
  • Public Awareness - Education campaigns
